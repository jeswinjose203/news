ELTD(Experience Learning Takeaway Discovery)

ELTD, or Experience Learning Takeaway Discovery, is a method of learning that emphasizes hands-on experiences and discovery-based learning. This approach allows learners to actively engage with the material and gain a deeper understanding of the subject matter. ELTD is particularly effective for subjects that require practical application, such as science, technology, engineering, and math. By using ELTD, learners can develop critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and a greater appreciation for the subject matter.
